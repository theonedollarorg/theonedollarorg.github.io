About Us

We, a group of friends, realised over a dinner conversation that all of us want to contribute in some way or the other to help make the world a better place. But each one of us on that table wanted to support a different cause. Also, the donations each one of us would make were like a teeny tiny drop in the ocean. That's when The One Dollar Org was thought of...each one of sets aside a small amount each month, we pool it together to form a substantial amount and donate it to a different cause each month. After doing this for a couple of months we decided to reach a larger audience to get the support we need.

We are just a bunch of youngsters who want to do some good. While all of us cannot physically participate, we can certainly help those doing the actual ground work. Come join us to give a helping hand to those who need it!

Being a part of us is easy and simple!

Our Work


Khaana Chahiye - COVID-19 Relief

We donated $181 in May 2020 which helped them distribute around 550 meals to the underprivileged people affected due to COVID-19 across Mumbai


ActionAid India - Cyclone Amphan

We donated $184 in June 2020 which helped them provide basic necessities (Medical supplies, Dry rations, Mosquito nets, COVID-19 protection gear, Solar lamp, Education kit for children, Sanitation kit and some household items) to one family out of the many families affected by Amphan Cyclone in West Bengal and Odisha


ActionAid India - Assam Floods

We donated $197 in July 2020 which helped 24 families affected by devastating floods in Assam with hygiene kits, 44 families with dry ration and 42 familes with mosquito nets, COVID-19 protection material and waterproof tents


Awaaz - The Voice of Stray Animals

We donated $211 in August 2020 which helped feed 15000 dogs since lockdown, and a couple of hundred cats. This money was also used to provide medical supplies for the same.


Aasra - Providing Mental Health

We donated $209 in September 2020 which helped them operate 24*7 suicide prevention hotline, increase awareness, conduct research and educate people to become first interventionist.


DonateKraft - Hyderabad Floods

We donated $201 in October 2020 which towards 17 grocery kits, 16 soaps, sanitary pads and tarpaulins needed for 200 families of those affected by Hyderabad Floods.


Goonj - Cyclone Nivar

This money ($201) was used directly by the Goonj NGO to access the situatio created by Cyclone Nivar in Chennai and Pondicherry. The NGO will also provide relief towards the families affected by the same.


Global Giving - Farmers

Our donation of $200 dollars is going to help 20 farmers in purchasing high quality seeds, setup fruit orchards and vegetable plantations.
